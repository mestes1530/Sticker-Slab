    Step By Step Guide For Sticker Slab
    1.) Create an account, you can create a Sticker Slab account by clicking here. A Sticker Slab account lets user's save slabs by bookmarking them as well as create their own Sticker Slabs.

    2.) Checkout all the created public slabs for inspiration by clicking on the Browse tab. Use the search bar to find a specific slab and the bookmark button on the bottom to save your favorite slabs.

    3.) Customize your own slabs by adding text, link and image stickers to share content on them. Since there is no feed, it is up to you update your slab with new stickers and delete old stickers you think they are just taking up space.

    4.) Copy one of your slab's links by pressing the "Edit" tab at the bottom of the slab then the button labeled "Copy Link". You can then share your slab's link with your friends so they can click on all the stickers to see the slab's content.

    About Sticker Slab
    Sticker Slab is a simple social media website that works to strip away the announces of other popular platforms. No feed forcing content in your face, no algorithm analysing your every click to determine the most effective advertisement to show you, and no creepy cyborg selling your data to corporations; just simply sticking post to digital slabs to share with whoever.
    Sticker Slab is a capstone project coded by Mitchell Estes while participating in PDX Code Guild's Full Stack Bootcamp.
    11/12/2018
